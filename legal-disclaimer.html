Legal And Product Disclaimer

Core disclaimer

Dealer Guard helps users inspect vehicle deal structure, negotiation tactics, promises, and contract mismatches. It does not replace careful document review by the buyer.

Not professional advice

  • Dealer Guard does not provide legal advice.
  • Dealer Guard does not provide tax advice.
  • Dealer Guard does not provide lending advice.
  • Dealer Guard does not provide credit repair advice.
  • Dealer Guard does not provide dealership compliance advice.

User-facing guidance

  • Use Dealer Guard as a second set of eyes before signing.
  • Confirm every important number on the written contract.
  • Do not rely on verbal promises if they are not reflected on paper.

Risk note

Estimates, savings counters, and what-if scenarios are directional tools. They are not guarantees of actual savings or financing approval.

Before public launch, have this language reviewed and aligned with your final privacy policy, store listing, and subscription terms.
